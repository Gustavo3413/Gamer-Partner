HENRY CAVILL IS CALLED TOXIC FOR WANTING THE WITCHER SERIES MORE FAITHFUL IN RELATION TO THE GAMES

E Beau DeMayo, ex-producer of The Witcher series, in a Q&A session in his Instagram, said that some screenwriters didn't like the books and games, and even mocked the original material. "I was in a series - namely The Witcher - where some screenwriters actively disliked the books and games, and kept mocking the original material. It's a recipe for disaster and low morale. The original material needs to be respected before anything can be added to its legacy" said DeMayo.
