PEDRO PASCAL IS AN EMMY NOMINEE FOR HIS PERFORMANCE IN THE LAST OF US AS JOEL

The 2023 Emmy nominees were revealed this month. The HBO series The Last of Us received no fewer than 24 nominations.

Pedro Pascal is this year’s Emmy overachiever, receiving three nominations, including one for ‘The Last of Us’ and another as guest host on ‘Saturday Night Live.’

HBO’s “The Last of Us” is the first live-action video game adaptation to earn major awards consideration from a top Hollywood awards body, in television or in film.

Pascal also earned a third nomination for his narration on CNN's Patagonia: Life on the Edge of the World, a documentary series about the diverse landscapes and lifeforms of the titular South American location. He'll square off against Mahershala Ali (Chimp Empire), Angela Bassett (Good Night Oppy), Morgan Freeman (Our Universe), and Barack Obama (Working: What We Do All Day).
