DETROIT: BECOME HUMAN REVIEW

Detroit: Become Human is a game produced by Quantic Dream and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment for PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows PC. The story focuses on Kara, Markus, and Connor, three androids conceived by the fictitious company CyberLife that, with its decisions, will change Detroit and the United States' history. Also, the game lets us observe the rise of a new race: The Deviants (androids that manifest human emotion).

Voxel gave the game a 91/100 (excellent) e stated that “Detroit: Become Human is a huge leap of what Heavy Rain was and brings a story and decisions well put together, something Beyond was lacking. A magnificent work from Quantic Dream”, but still criticized that “The game follows the gameplay formula from Quantic Dream and does not innovate (and movement control are still painful).

GAMER PARTNER COMMENT:

"The game is very immersive. It looks like you are watching a movie worthy of an Oscar. Having your choices defining the rest of the story so well make you want to watch the game again and again just to watch all possible outcomes and endings. It never gets boring."
